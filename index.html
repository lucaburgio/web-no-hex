Turn 1 Phase: Production

Instructions

You (P) = south border
AI (A) = north border

Production: click any hex on the bottom row to place a unit.

Movement: click one of your units to select it (highlighted), then click an adjacent hex to move. Click "End Movement" when done.

Combat: move onto an enemy unit to attack. ATK 1 vs DEF 2 — attacker loses on tie.
