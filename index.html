    Game Rules

    Overview

    Turn-based hex strategy on a 6×4 grid. You play from the south (bottom row); the AI plays from the north (top row).

    Turn Phases

    1. Production — spend PP to place units.
    2. Movement — move each of your units up to 1 hex.
    3. End — AI takes its turn, then the turn counter advances.

    Production

    • Each turn you earn 20 PP (production points).
    • Territory bonus: +2 PP for every 10 hexes you own.
    • Spend 20 PP to place an Infantry unit.
    • Valid placement hexes: your home row (bottom), or any production hex you own.
    • Production hex: an owned hex that has been stable for 2 consecutive turns. Stability requires all hexes within distance 2 to be owned by you (no neutral, no enemy). Stability resets immediately if that condition breaks.
    • You can place multiple units per turn as long as you have PP.

    Movement

    • Each unit may move 1 hex per turn.
    • Moving onto an empty hex conquers it (converts it to your territory).
    • Moving onto an enemy unit triggers combat.
    • Zone of Control (ZoC): if your unit is adjacent to an enemy, it is "locked." It may only attack an adjacent enemy or retreat to a hex that is not itself adjacent to any enemy. It cannot freely move away to a safe hex.

    Combat

    • Both sides deal damage simultaneously.
    • Combat Strength (CS) = base strength (10) × condition × flanking bonus.
    • Condition: scales from 50% (1 HP) to 100% (full HP).
    • Flanking: each additional friendly unit adjacent to the defender grants +15% CS to the attacker (max 2 flankers = +30%).
    • Damage formula: floor(3 × exp(±ΔCS / 10)), minimum 1 damage per side.
    • If the defender dies: attacker advances onto that hex and conquers it.
    • If both die: both units are removed.
    • Hover over an enemy unit during movement to see a combat forecast.

    Healing

    • Units that did not fight this turn heal at end of turn.
    • +2 HP on own territory, +1 HP on neutral, +0 HP on enemy territory.

    Victory

    • Move a unit onto the opponent's home row, or eliminate all enemy units.
