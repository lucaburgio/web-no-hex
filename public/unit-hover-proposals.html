Unit hover — board silhouette + HP + icon

Same path as in renderer.ts (UNIT_PATH_D), HP bar layout, and a sample icons/units/infantry.svg. Hover the whole piece — shape, bar, and icon move together. 1–10 are the first batch; 11–20 add another ladder from subtle to extreme.

1 · Subtle Brightness + 2px lift Quiet feedback; good for a dense board.
2 · Soft Blue glow Highlights interactivity without changing size.
3 · Minimal accent Accent drop-shadow Reads like a selection stripe under the unit.
4 · Classic Scale 108% Common tactics-game affordance.
5 · Medium Pulsing stroke on shield Extra “selectable” signal when tiles are busy.
6 · Strong Lift + shadow + glow Strong depth; pops off the hex.
7 · Playful Spring overshoot Bouncy; fits lighter tone.
8 · Loud Wiggle Hard to miss; noisy if every unit does it.
9 · Very loud Endless pulse + glow While hovered only; still strong.
10 · Extreme Rainbow stroke + spin + bloom Joke / debug tier — not for default gameplay.
11 · Subtle Skew + tiny scale Slight diagonal lean; feels tactile without growing much.
12 · Soft Saturation + contrast Color “wakes up”; zero translation — good if motion is restricted.
13 · Minimal Hairline white rim Crisp focus ring on the shield only (stroke path under fill).
14 · Medium 3D card tilt Rotation + offset shadow; reads like picking up a token.
15 · Calm motion Floating bob Smooth up/down loop while hovered — noticeable but not frantic.
16 · Strong rhythm Double heartbeat Two quick beats then rest — grabs attention without endless vibration.
17 · Playful Jelly squash & stretch Non-uniform scale wobble; cartoon / mobile energy.
18 · Loud Marching “ants” outline Animated dashed stroke; very “tooling / selection” vibes.
19 · Very loud Chromatic split + scale Offset RGB shadows; glitchy without spinning.
20 · Extreme Hue dance + blur + jitter scale Overload tier for comparison — not a shipping default.
